October 19-21, 2022 // Tyumen
On October 20, 2022, the General Board of the Union of Russian Cities will be held in the city of Tyumen.

The main goal of this Board is to build additional contacts between Russian cities and colleagues from the cities of the CIS, EAEU, BRICS, SCO member countries for cooperation in the economic, scientific, technical, humanitarian and other fields.

Within the Board, the “International Forum of Local Governments: “Cities of the Present and Future” will be held with the support of the Alexander Gorchakov Public Diplomacy Fund, the Administration of Tyumen and the Government of the Tyumen region.

Dear colleagues, we invite you to take part in the Board of the Union of Russian Cities and the International Forum of Local Governments.
